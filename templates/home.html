Who are we ?

“Aster” is a non-profit organization that collaborates with schools in order to build inclusive, comforting and safe clubs for teenagers 12 - 18, who suffer from family violence.The clubs provide counseling, a chance to bond with other teenagers in similar situations , and a wide variety of activities and skills that are both fun and are proven to increase happiness and well-being.

Let us explain

We present in schools, teenagers can contact us in various ways, we arrange counselors to have discussions with the students privately after these one on one discussions the counselor can get a good idea of the teenagers problem and she can suggest the club to him, teenagers who have minor problems which are not domestic abuse can keep coming to the counselor with keeping contact information. The club will begin at schools as it is the safest way to allow the teenagers to stay after school and have activities but there will be a center club which we can arrange the full experience of the program in. In the program we have therapists and other students who have come to the program, we have talking sessions with the group and also private, we do fun activities and teach them skills that are unique(more on that in the description of the club section).

• App Development •

The Aster app provides an easy and safe way for teens to communicate, either with therapists or other teenagers. A simple random class code is given out in our clubs for privacy, which when typed in, will give access to communicate with the teenagers and therapists in​ the Aster club you've joined. Additionally there are a number of statistics and articles that can be accessed from the app, to emphasize the severity of the family violence issue.
