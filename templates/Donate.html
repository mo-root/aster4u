WHY ASTER?

Aster takes the steps required in the direction which ensures that the world's youth and younger generations will not have to go through the horrific experience of family violence. By introducing aster in schools, incorporating therapists and activities in the Aster clubs for the teenagers who suffer from family violence, aster can build a brighter future.Aster maintains its presence in schools as well, not only for therapy across the school’s students but for the increase of knowledge on the topic of family violence, in order to encourage teenagers all across the world to stand up and take action against family violence.
